About Us

TextbookConsultants connects students, parents, and instructors to competitive and market-efficient price of textbooks by presenting the data publicly available.

We remember our days in college when we or our classmates had to take classes without books because the books were too expensive.

There is much difference in learning with and without having a textbook. In some disciplines, students cannot be expected to know the subject matter without having a textbook.

Our hope is that our work serves students, future intellectuals of our society, along with parents and instructors by:

  1. Reducing textbook searching time and cost for college students, who have other priorities ahead.
  2. Presenting class and textbook lists in an unrestricted format so that students can easily see and compare textbooks outside and beyond the current classes.
  3. Presenting competitive and market-efficient prices of textbooks.
  4. Analyzing trends in textbook information and prices used across schools and classes.

Thank you for joining our mission and we wish you a great semester.



-TextbookConsultants Team
