Special Section on Hurricane Harvey - OnwardFromHarvey

A disastrous hurricane has struck our town in the upper and middle part of Houston. Thanks to many great volunteers and organizations, we are recovering from the damages of the hurricane, but we have more works to do.

The damage to Houston does not end with the hurricane or the flood. For years on, we Houstonians will need to fight microorganisms, adverse species, harmful chemicals and elements, diminished infrastructure, and more. We will use any and every help to eliminate these threats and restore Houston to better than it was before.

The damage from the Hurricane Harvey is not confined to Houston. Whenever possible, we hope to reach out to neighbor cities and do the same work we do for Houston.

We will document and update on all our activities and steps taken to improve Houstoninan and surrounding communities.

We will start our work from the Northside and the Downtown Houston, where our agents are located, and we hope to continue our work in other parts of the city and the state.

Specifically, the current top priorities for us now is to disinfect microorganisms and insects such as mosquitoes in the standing pools that might pose health hazard to the people.

We will update the page with all the purchases we make and activities we engage in with respect to recovery from Harvey.
