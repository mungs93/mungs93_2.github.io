Conclusion


Using a new book with the same edition from the market saves 57.8322146118721 USD or 40.2398655864406 percent on average.

Using a used book with the same edition from the market saves 66.0462100456621 USD or 54.1279182921322 percent on average.

Using a new book with one lower edition from the market saves 92.1328070175439 USD or 53.7194073391013 percent on average.

Using a used book with the lower edition from the market saves 115.711303258145 USD or 76.0062516424261 percent on average.
